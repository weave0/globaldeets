About the mission

Our Story & Mission

A Living Window into Our Planet

GlobalDeets exists because the world's most important data is already public — it just isn't accessible. We connect curious humans to authoritative primary sources, live news, and a global ecosystem of knowledge.

The Mission

GlobalDeets is not another news aggregator. It's a planetary interface — a place where every data point connects to its primary source, every headline links to a real human story, and every map pin anchors you in real geography.

We believe an informed planet is a better planet. We believe curiosity is a public good. And we believe the barrier between a human and the world's knowledge should be exactly zero — no paywalls, no logins, no dark patterns.

Everything here is built in the open: open source data, primary sources, public archives, and free APIs. If we use someone's data, we link back to the original. Always.

Core Principles

Primary Sources Only

Every data point traces back to its original institution — UN, NASA, WHO, or equivalent. No laundering through aggregators.

Zero Barriers

No accounts. No paywalls. No cookies popups beyond what's required by law. Information is a right, not a product.

Human-Scale Design

Built for real humans on real devices. Accessibility-first, performance-first, clarity-first. No dark patterns. No outrage loops.

Radical Transparency

Open-source frontend. Public build pipeline. If we make a mistake, we document it. CHANGELOG.md and all.

Geography is Context

Every story has a location. Every statistic has a geography. The globe is the default view because place matters.

Everything Is Connected

News, culture, climate, health, rights — they're not separate tabs. They're facets of one world. We build with that in mind.

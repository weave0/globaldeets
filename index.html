Source Covenant

The rules that keep GlobalDeets useful as it grows from a live news surface into a planetary catalog.

Original Source First

Headlines, cultural references, statistics, and public datasets link outward to the institution or publisher that owns the record.

No Synthetic Spin

We organize, translate, label, and route. We do not rewrite world events into our own opinion layer.

Freshness Is Visible

Feeds should show last update, cache age, source health, and failure states instead of pretending everything is current.

Daily Human Value

Every page should help a visitor understand a place, follow a source, learn a culture, or leave with a useful thread to return to.
